The 2017 Lineup

Northwest

September 6th, 2017

Nike World Headquarters
Beaverton, Oregon

Join us on September 7th, 2017 for AgileCamp Northwest in beautiful Beaverton Oregon! Agilists from across the Pacific Northwest will converge at the Nike World Headquarters for a career changing day of presentations and workshops. Nike is a leader in Agile project management and a proud sponsor of AgileCamp. This year we are excited for keynote presentations from Nintendo CEO Tom Nook and Hip Hop mogul Jay Z!
